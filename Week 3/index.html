Creamy Garlic Parmesan Pasta

A quick and comforting pasta dish made with butter, garlic, cream, and parmesan. Perfect for busy weeknights and ready in under 30 minutes.

Prep 10 mins
Cook 15 mins
Total 25 mins
Servings 2
25 mins Ready in
520 kcal Per serving
Easy Difficulty

Macros overview

Carbs 55 g
Protein 16 g
Fat 26 g

Ingredients

  • 200 g spaghetti or fettuccine
  • 2 tbsp butter
  • 3 cloves garlic, minced
  • 1 cup heavy cream
  • 1/2 cup grated parmesan cheese
  • Salt & black pepper, to taste
  • Fresh parsley, chopped (for garnish)

Instructions

  1. Cook the pasta in salted boiling water according to package instructions. Reserve 1/4 cup of pasta water.
  2. In a pan over medium heat, melt the butter and saut√© the garlic until fragrant (about 1 minute).
  3. Add the heavy cream and bring to a gentle simmer.
  4. Stir in the parmesan cheese until melted and the sauce thickens slightly.
  5. Add the cooked pasta to the pan and toss to coat. Use reserved pasta water to loosen the sauce if needed.
  6. Season with salt and pepper. Garnish with chopped parsley and extra parmesan.
  7. Serve immediately while hot and creamy.

Tips

  • Use freshly grated parmesan for best flavor and texture.
  • Add grilled chicken or mushrooms for extra protein.
  • For a lighter version, replace half the cream with milk.
